How TrafficSense Works

Our system uses real-time data processing to calculate traffic density. Here is how we do it:

  • Data Collection: Gathering live location data points.
  • Processing: Our Java-powered backend analyzes the volume of active signals in a given area.
  • Routing: We provide you with the clearest path to your destination.
